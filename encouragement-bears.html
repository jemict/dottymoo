What this is

Encouragement Bears are personalised soft toy keepsakes designed to carry a meaningful message. Each bear includes a name printed on the front of the T-shirt and a supportive message printed on the back. They are designed for thoughtful gifting, comfort, encouragement and reassurance.

Personalisation

  • Name printed on the front of the T-shirt
  • Supportive message chosen from a category below for the back of the T-shirt
  • Message style includes Dotty Moo colours and signature printed design
