For Family Attractions

Information for farms, country parks and family-friendly attractions interested in offering a personalised mascot bear.

Dotty Moo mascot bears are designed as a gentle keepsake from a special day out. Each bear is printed with your logo and colours, helping children take a little piece of the experience home with them.

We keep things simple and low risk. Most attractions start with a single bear design so you can see how it feels in your setting, with the option to build from there only if it makes sense for your visitors.

  1. We create a sample. We prepare a draft design using your logo and colours, and can provide a sample bear so you can visualise how it will look in your shop.
  2. You decide how to trial it. Choose a small, sensible starting quantity and where it will sit in your gift shop or farm shop. We support a calm, straightforward setup without pressure.
  3. We deliver to your venue. Bears are produced together and delivered to your attraction, ready to be displayed and offered as a keepsake.

There is no need to hold large stock or commit to a wide range. The focus is on fit, quality and a calm visitor experience.

All Dotty Moo bears are fully safety tested and designed for children.
