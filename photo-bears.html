Photo bear FAQ

How do I send my photo?

After placing your order, email your photo to hello@dottymoo.co.uk and include your order number. Clear, well-lit images work best for printing.

What type of photo works best?

High resolution, bright images with the subject clearly visible produce the best results. Avoid screenshots or heavily cropped images where possible.

Can I add text as well as a photo?

Yes. You can include a short name or message alongside your photo on the T-shirt design.

Will you check the image before printing?

Yes. Each order is checked before printing. If there are any issues with image quality, we will contact you before proceeding.

Is this suitable as a gift?

Yes. Photo bears are popular for birthdays, anniversaries and family keepsakes. You can send your order directly to the recipient at checkout.
