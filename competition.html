Mega Kids Digital Hamper Competition

Our competitions are currently on pause. In the meantime, you can browse our personalised bears collection for thoughtful keepsake gifts.

The Dotty Moo Mega Kids Digital Hamper is a half-termly, skills-based competition for UK families. One winner will receive a digital prize bundle worth over £100, only £2.50 per entry. Dotty Moo Ltd will make a donation equivalent to 20% of entry fees, capped at £100, to a UK school nominated by the winner. A quick, safe and family-friendly competition for UK families.

This competition’s prize

  • £30 Gaming Credit (Xbox · PlayStation · Roblox)
  • £25 App Store Credit (Google Play · Apple)
  • £25 Disney+ Credit
  • £20 National Book Token
  • Dotty Moo Printable Activity Pack

The full prize is delivered digitally by email to the winner.

How to enter

  1. Purchase your £2.50 entry on our secure competition page.
  2. Answer one simple but fair skill question during checkout.

Entries that are incomplete, unpaid, incorrect, or submitted after the closing date will not be entered into the draw.

Entries close on Sunday 1st February 2026 at 8:00pm (UK time).

The winner will be drawn and announced the following day once all valid entries and bonus entries have been verified.

Bonus entry (optional)

Bonus entries are available via our pinned Facebook competition post during the promotional period. One optional bonus entry is available to participants who have already purchased a valid entry, if they complete all of the following:

  • Follows the Dotty Moo social media page
  • Shares the competition post to their story or public feed so it can be seen
  • Comment with the name of the school you would like to nominate on this post once complete

All actions must be completed to qualify for the bonus entry.

No social media action is required to enter the competition.

This promotion is in no way sponsored, endorsed, administered by or associated with Facebook or Instagram.

School donation
