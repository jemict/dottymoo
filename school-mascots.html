School Mascot Bears

Information for schools interested in offering Dotty Moo mascot bears, with two simple ordering routes.

Our personalised mascot bears help children feel safe, settled and connected, while celebrating school identity and community. Each bear is printed with your logo and colours, creating a keepsake children love.

Choose your ordering route

Both options are calm, organised and designed to fit around school life. Choose the route that works best for your school community.

Route 1: Parent ordering

  • Families order using a school specific link
  • School chooses a clear ordering window
  • We handle payments and customer queries
  • One bulk delivery direct to school

Best for: community ordering, leavers, new starters, PTA support, general availability.

Route 2: Direct orders (wholesale)

  • School orders directly at wholesale pricing
  • Simple ordering and invoicing
  • Ideal for planned school use
  • Delivered to school in one shipment

Best for: rewards, nurture, attendance, wellbeing initiatives, budgets and structured distribution.

Common school uses

  • Rewards and recognition
  • Nurture and wellbeing
  • Attendance and kindness awards
  • Transition and new starter gifts

Enquire Bear details

Route 1: Parent ordering (school link)

Dotty Moo works directly with schools to offer a simple, secure ordering window for families. There is no upfront cost for schools, and no stock to manage.

  1. We set up your school. We create a private ordering page using your school logo and colours, and provide ready-to-use parent communication.
  2. You share the ordering link. Families order and pay securely during your chosen ordering window.
  3. We deliver to your school. All bears are produced together and delivered in one bulk order, ready for pupils.

Route 2: Direct orders (wholesale)

If you prefer to purchase mascot bears directly, we offer a straightforward direct order route at wholesale pricing. This works well when you want a planned quantity for school use.

  1. We confirm your design. You share your logo and preferred colours. We confirm the bear design before production.
  2. You place your order. We provide clear ordering details and invoicing information.
  3. We deliver to school. Your order is produced and delivered in one shipment, ready for you to use as needed.
