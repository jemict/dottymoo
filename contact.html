Contact

If you would like to get in touch about Dotty Moo, you are very welcome to contact us. The quickest way to reach us is by email.

Email: hello@dottymoo.co.uk
We aim to respond within two working days.

For families

If you have a question about a personalised bear, an order you have placed, or choosing the right option, please email and we will be happy to help.

If your message is about an existing order, including your order name and the email address used at checkout helps us respond more quickly.

For schools

If you are a school interested in ordering Dotty Moo mascot bears at wholesale pricing for your setting, please get in touch.

It is helpful if you include your school name, postcode, and an estimated quantity, even a rough guide is fine.

For organisations

If you run a farm, attraction, club, charity, museum, or other organisation and would like to explore a personalised mascot bear or keepsake for your visitors or community, we would love to hear from you.

We usually start with a simple conversation and, where helpful, a sample so you can see how it feels in your setting. There is no obligation and no pressure to proceed.
