Privacy Policy

We take privacy seriously. This summary explains what we collect, why, and your choices. This is general guidance and not legal advice.

Who we are

Dotty Moo Ltd (“we”, “us”) is a UK-based business providing school keepsakes and related services. Contact: hello@dottymoo.co.uk.

We are registered with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO). Registration number: ZC049916.

What we collect

  • Order information (via your school’s private link): pupil name, class, chosen product, and payment confirmation.
  • Contact information if you email us.
  • Technical data (privacy-friendly analytics).

How we use data

  • To process and deliver school orders
  • To provide support and respond to enquiries
  • To improve our website and service

Lawful bases

  • Contract for orders placed during a school’s order window
  • Legitimate interests for analytics and service improvement
  • Consent where required

Sharing

We share only what’s necessary with service providers (e.g., payment processors) and your school for fulfilment. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Order records are kept for as long as needed for fulfilment, accounting, and legal purposes, then securely deleted or anonymised.

Your rights

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability (where applicable)
  • To exercise rights, email sales@dottymoo.co.uk
  • Complain to the ICO: ico.org.uk

Children’s data

Orders are coordinated through schools and placed by parents/guardians. We do not market directly to pupils.

Cookies & analytics

We use minimal, privacy-friendly analytics. You can block cookies in your browser settings.
