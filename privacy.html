Privacy Policy

We take privacy seriously. This summary explains what we collect, why, and your choices. This is general guidance and not legal advice.

Who we are

Dotty Moo (“we”, “us”) is a UK-based business providing school keepsakes and related services. You can contact us at sales@dottymoo.co.uk.

What we collect

  • Order information (via your school’s private link): pupil name, class, chosen product, and payment confirmation.
  • Contact information if you email us.
  • Technical data (basic analytics such as pages visited, device or browser).

How we use data

  • To process and deliver school orders
  • To provide support and respond to enquiries
  • To improve our website and service

Lawful bases

  • Contract to fulfil orders placed through a school’s ordering window
  • Legitimate interests for privacy-friendly analytics and service improvement
  • Consent where required

Sharing

We share only what is necessary with service providers such as payment processors and your school for order fulfilment. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Order records are kept for as long as needed for fulfilment, accounting, and legal requirements, then securely deleted or anonymised.

Your rights

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability where applicable
  • To exercise rights, email sales@dottymoo.co.uk
  • You can complain to the ICO: ico.org.uk

Children’s data

Orders are coordinated through schools and placed by parents or guardians. We do not market directly to pupils.

Cookies and analytics

We use minimal, privacy-friendly analytics to understand site usage. You can adjust your browser to block cookies.
