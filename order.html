Order through your school

Dotty Moo orders are available only via your child's school during its official window.

Ready to order? Please use the QR code or link provided on the letter from your child's school. That will take you to your school's private page to personalise and pay securely. If you haven’t received a letter, contact your school to confirm dates for the next window.

What your school order could look like

There are no public orders on this website. This protects schools and keeps the process simple for families.
