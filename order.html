Families

Information for parents and carers ordering through their child’s school.

If you are visiting from a school and would like to offer Dotty Moo mascot bears, please get in touch via our contact page.

Ready to order? Please use the QR code or link provided on the letter from your school. This will take you to your school’s private ordering page where you can place your order and pay securely. If you haven’t received a letter, please contact your school.
