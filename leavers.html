Leavers’ Graduation Bears

A simple, personalised keepsake to celebrate school leavers. Each bear is printed with your school logo and optional year group, then supplied ready for end-of-year events.

Schools can choose either a single wholesale bulk order, or a fully managed parent ordering window with delivery to school.

Two ways to order

Option 1: Wholesale bulk order

One simple school order, supplied in a single batch, ready for your ceremony or leavers’ events.

No artwork fees, no delivery charges, no VAT

50–99 bears – £6.40
100+ bears – £5.90
Minimum order: 20 bears

Quick questions

Do you deliver to home addresses?

Leavers’ ordering is normally delivered to school in one batch for simplicity. If you need an alternative arrangement, please contact us.

How long does it take?

We confirm timings when your order is agreed. Parent ordering pages include a closing date and an estimated delivery week.

Can we use a different year group name?

Yes. The print can be adapted to suit your leavers year group and wording.
