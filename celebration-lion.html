Checkout will ask for celebration message, name and optional celebration icon. For the best print size and clarity, we recommend keeping each line to around 20 characters. Longer messages may be resized to fit the available print area.

  • Celebration Message: Your wording (e.g. Happy Birthday, You did it!, Well done, So proud of you, Congratulations)
  • Name: Name printed on T-shirt
  • Celebration Icon & Accent Colour: Enter the icon code (A–M) and your chosen colour.

Example:
Celebration Message: Happy Birthday
Name: Charlie
Celebration Icon & Accent Colour: A - Blue

Your text will be printed exactly as entered.
