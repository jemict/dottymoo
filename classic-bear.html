How to personalise at checkout

Checkout will ask for three lines. You can use them in any order.
For the best print size and clarity, we recommend keeping each line to around 20 characters. Longer messages may be resized to fit the available print area.

  • Line 1: your text (optional)
  • Line 2: your text (optional)
  • Line 3: style notes (optional, for example: font, colour, icon code)

Example:
Line 1: Missing you! (script/blue)
Line 2: Charlie (soft rounded/grey)
Line 3: C3 (blue)

Please note: Anything written in brackets is for guidance only and will not be printed on the bear.
