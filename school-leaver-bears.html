What this is

School Leaver Bears are personalised soft toys created as a meaningful keepsake for children leaving nursery, primary school, or another educational setting.

Each bear is designed to feel familiar, comforting, and special, something children can keep as they move on to their next stage.

Who this may suit

  • Children finishing nursery, preschool or primary school
  • Families looking for a meaningful end-of-year keepsake
  • Schools, PTAs or settings organising leaver gifts

Personalisation

You can personalise the T-shirt with the child’s details and either a school name or a school logo.

What you will enter at checkout

  • Child’s name
  • Year (for example: 2026)
  • School name OR school logo

If you choose school name, just type the school name at checkout.

If you choose school logo, place your order first, then email the logo to hello@dottymoo.co.uk. Please include your order name (and order number if available).

How they are used

Leaver bears are often given at the end of term, during leavers assemblies, or as a small gift from home to mark a big transition.

They work equally well as individual gifts or as part of a coordinated school or class order.
