School Leaver Bears

A personalised keepsake bear designed to mark the end of a school chapter and celebrate moving on.

What this is

School Leaver Bears are personalised soft toys created as a meaningful keepsake for children leaving a nursery, primary school, or other educational setting.

Each bear is designed to feel familiar, comforting, and special — something children can keep as they move on to their next stage.

Who this may suit

  • Children finishing nursery, preschool or primary school
  • Families looking for a meaningful end-of-year keepsake
  • Schools, PTAs or settings organising leaver gifts

How it is personalised

  • Child’s name
  • School or setting name
  • Year or leavers message
  • Optional symbols or icons

Product details

  • Product size: 20cm sitting height (sits unaided)
  • Materials: made from recycled plastics
  • Safety: CE and UKCA marked, conforms to EN71 toy safety standards

How they are used

Leaver bears are often given at the end of term, during leavers assemblies, or as a small gift from home to mark a big transition.

They work equally well as individual gifts or as part of a coordinated school or class order.
