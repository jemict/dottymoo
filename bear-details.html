Dotty Moo Mascot Bears – Product Details

Dotty Moo mascot bears are personalised keepsakes created for UK schools and their pupils. Each bear is designed to be safe, durable, and meaningful, whether it is used as part of school life or kept at home as a reminder of special memories.

All Dotty Moo mascot bears are fully safety tested and compliant with UK toy safety standards. This ensures they are suitable for use in schools and safe for children to enjoy at home.

Key Information

  • Size: 22 cm sitting height, 27 cm head to toe (sits unaided)
  • Included: Soft plush bear with white t-shirt printed in full colour with your school logo
  • Print area: 9 cm wide × 8.3 cm high
  • Artwork set up: Free – full artwork support included
  • Colour: Classic soft brown (our standard Dotty Moo bear)

Materials & Safety

  • Soft plush fabric with embroidered facial features and paws
  • Fibre filled and durable, designed as a long lasting keepsake
  • CE / UKCA marked
  • Conforms to EN71 Toy Safety Standards
  • Age recommendation: Suitable for all ages

Why Schools Love Them

Our mascot bears offer one of the largest print areas available on a compact bear, so your school logo is bright, clear and stands out beautifully. Bears arrive ready to hand out, creating a memorable keepsake that families treasure.
