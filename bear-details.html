Dotty Moo Mascot Bears – Product Details

Dotty Moo mascot bears are personalised keepsakes created for UK schools and family-friendly settings. Each bear is thoughtfully designed to be safe, durable and meaningful, whether it becomes part of everyday school life or a keepsake from a special day out, treasured at home.

All Dotty Moo mascot bears are fully safety tested and compliant with UK toy safety standards, making them suitable for use in educational settings and safe for children to enjoy at home.

Key Information

  • Size: 22 cm sitting height, 27 cm head to toe (sits unaided)
  • Included: Soft plush bear with white t-shirt printed in full colour with your school logo
  • Print area: 9 cm wide × 8.3 cm high
  • Artwork set up: Free – full artwork support included
  • Colour: Classic soft brown (our standard Dotty Moo bear)

Materials & Safety

  • Soft plush fabric with embroidered facial features and paws
  • Fibre filled and durable, designed as a long lasting keepsake
  • CE / UKCA marked
  • Conforms to EN71 Toy Safety Standards
  • Age recommendation: Suitable for all ages

Example mascot bear shown.

Why these bears work so well

Dotty Moo mascot bears offer a generous print area on a compact, child-friendly bear, allowing logos and designs to appear clear, bright and easy to recognise. Each bear arrives ready to share and is designed to become a lasting keepsake, treasured by children and families at home.
