Schools & Organisations

For schools and organisations who want to place a single bulk order at wholesale pricing.

Dotty Moo mascot bears are designed as gentle keepsakes from meaningful moments and experiences. Each bear is personalised with your logo and colours, helping children take a small piece of the place or occasion home with them.

We keep things simple and low risk. Most settings start with a single bear design, so you can see how it works before deciding whether to build from there.

  1. We create a sample. We prepare a draft design using your logo and colours, with the option of a sample bear.
  2. You choose how to trial it. Start with a small, sensible quantity and see how it works in your setting.
  3. We deliver to your venue. Bears arrive ready to display and share as a keepsake.

There is no need to hold large stock or commit to a wide range. The focus is on fit, quality and a calm experience for your setting.
