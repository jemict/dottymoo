What this is

Identity, Belonging & Acceptance Bears are personalised soft toy keepsakes designed to carry a meaningful message. Each bear includes a name printed on the front of the T-shirt and an identity-focused message printed on the back. They are designed as thoughtful gifts for reassurance, encouragement and everyday support.

Personalisation

  • Name printed on the front of the T-shirt
  • Identity, belonging or acceptance message printed on the back of the T-shirt
