What this is

Monkey Hugs is a simple, reassuring keepsake soft toy designed to be a familiar comfort item at home, or when out and about.

It is not a medical product, but it can be a helpful part of a calm routine, a transition, or a “comfort item” choice when a child wants a quiet reset.

Who this may suit

  • Children who benefit from familiar comfort items
  • Families building calm routines (bedtime, travel, transitions)
  • Anyone who loves playful character toys and silly smiles

How it is used

Many families keep a comfort soft toy in a predictable place and offer it as a choice when a child wants a quiet reset.
