Newborn bear FAQ

What can I personalise on a newborn bear?

You can personalise the name on the T-shirt, with your choice of accent colour and an optional icon. Many customers also add baby details such as date, time, weight and length.

Is this suitable for a newborn baby?

These bears are suitable from birth and are CE and UKCA marked to meet UK toy safety standards. For very young babies, always supervise and follow the product safety guidance supplied with your order.

Can I send this as a gift directly to new parents?

Yes. You can enter the recipient’s delivery address at checkout. Double check spelling for names and baby details before placing your order.
