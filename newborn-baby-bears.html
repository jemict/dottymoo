What this is

Newborn baby bears are a keepsake gift designed for new babies and growing families. After choosing your bear style, you can personalise the T-shirt with a baby name and optional details.

Personalisation

  • Name printed on the T-shirt
  • Font, colour and optional icon chosen after selecting your bear style
  • Optional baby details (for example: date, time, weight, length)

You will choose your exact wording after selecting your bear.
