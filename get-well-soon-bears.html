What this is

Get Well Soon Bears are personalised soft toys made as a thoughtful, reassuring gift. They are designed to feel simple and comforting, with a calm printed T-shirt message and optional personalisation.

They are not medical products, but they can be a gentle keepsake to help someone feel remembered and supported.

Personalisation

  • Get well message (for example: “Get well soon”, “Thinking of you”, or your own wording)
  • Name (optional)
  • Font, colour and optional icon chosen after selecting your bear style

Who this may suit

  • Children or adults who are unwell or recovering
  • Hospital stays, rest days, or quiet recovery at home
  • Friends and family looking for a meaningful “thinking of you” gift
