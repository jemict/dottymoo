Create your own bear FAQ

How do I personalise my teddy bear?

First choose your base bear above. On the product page you can select your font, colour and optional icon, then enter your chosen name or message at checkout.

How much text can I add?

We recommend keeping wording short so it prints clearly on the T-shirt. A name and short message works best. Exact character guidance is shown on each product page.

Can I add a photo as well?

If you would like to add a printed photo, please visit our Photo Bears page instead.

Are these personalised in the UK?

Yes. Each bear is personalised to order in the UK and checked before printing.

How long does personalisation take?

Orders are processed promptly after checkout. For urgent gifts, please contact us before ordering and we will do our best to help.
