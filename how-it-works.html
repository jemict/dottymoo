How it works

Dotty Moo partners with schools. Parents order only when a school's window is open.

  1. 1) Your school launches. Look out for a letter or email from your child's school. It will include a QR code or link.
  2. 2) Scan the QR code. Scan the code to visit your school's private page. Personalise your bear with your child's name and class.
  3. 3) Pay securely. Complete payment on the secure checkout. We prepare your school's bulk delivery so every bear arrives safely and ready to make pupils smile.
