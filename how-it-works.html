How it works

Dotty Moo partners with schools. Parents order only when a school's window is open.

  1. Your school launches. Look out for a letter or email from your child's school (QR code or link).
  2. Scan the QR code. Go to your school's private page and order your school's mascot bear.
  3. Pay securely. Complete checkout. We deliver a single bulk order to school so every bear arrives together for pupils.
