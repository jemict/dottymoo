What this is

Bear Hugs is a simple, reassuring keepsake bear designed to be a familiar comfort item at home, or when out and about.

It is not a medical product, but it can be a helpful part of a calm routine, a transition, or a “comfort item” as part of a calm routine at home.

Who this may suit

  • Children who benefit from familiar comfort items
  • Families building calm routines (bedtime, travel, transitions)

How it is used

Many families keep a comfort bear in a predictable place and offer it as a choice when a child wants a quiet reset.

How to personalise at checkout

Checkout will ask for name and accent colour. For the best print size and clarity, we recommend names to around 12 characters. Longer names may be resized to fit the available print area.

  • Name: Name to be printed
  • Accent Colour: Used for the top message and matching icon

Example:
Name: Charlie
Accent Colour: Pink

Your text will be printed exactly as entered.
Please check spelling, spacing, and capitalisation carefully before checkout.

Ordering more than one?

If you’re buying multiple bears, please list each bear’s details clearly in the personalisation box (for example: Bear 1 – Charlie / Bear 2 – Amelia).
