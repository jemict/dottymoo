How to personalise at checkout

Checkout will ask for three lines. You can use them in any order.
For the best print size and clarity, we recommend keeping each line to around 20 characters. Longer messages may be resized to fit the available print area.

  • Celebration Message: your text
  • Name: Name printed on T-shirt
  • Celebration Icon & Accent Colour: Choose A - M & accent colour

Example:
Celebration Message: Happy Birthday
Name: Charlie
Celebration Icon & Accent Colour: A - Blue

Your text will be printed exactly as entered.
Please check spelling, spacing, and capitalisation carefully before checkout.
