For Families

Information for parents and carers ordering through their child’s school.

Many families choose a school mascot bear as a familiar comfort item that helps children feel safe, settled and connected.

If you are visiting from a school and would like to offer Dotty Moo mascot bears, please get in touch via our contact page.

What to look out for

Schools send home a letter like this when an order window opens. Yours may look slightly different, but the format will be familiar.

Please use the QR code or link provided on the letter from your school.

This will take you to your school’s private ordering page where you can place your order and pay securely. If you haven’t received a letter, please contact your school.
