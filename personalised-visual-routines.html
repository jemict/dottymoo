Personalised Visual Routines

A calm, personalised set of visual routine boards created for your child. Designed to support predictability and reduce overwhelm at home.

Designed for your child, not a template

Many visual routines are designed to work for everyone. But children do not all process information in the same way.

These routine boards are created specifically for your child, based on how they understand language, cope with transitions, and move through their day. They are not templates or generic downloads.

Examples

These are sample layouts to show style and format. Your child’s routine will be personalised.

Where helpful, familiar words or phrases already used by your child can be included, and routines are written in clear UK English. This helps the visuals feel more recognisable and easier for your child to engage with.

Each routine is personalised using the information you share, including your child’s age, communication level, key challenges, and any non-negotiables that need to be included.

You can request a single routine board if that is what your child needs, or choose the full set if consistency across the day would be helpful.

How it is personalised

To personalise your routine board(s), I will ask for:

  • Your child’s first name
  • Which routine board you would like (morning, afternoon / after-school, bedtime), or all three
  • The steps you want to include (up to a maximum of 7 per routine board), or a short description of your routine so I can extract the steps for you
  • Preferred visual style: illustration, icon, or real-life photo
  • Label style: no label, one word, or a short phrase
  • Communication level: how your child understands language (single words, short action phrases, or very clear, explicit wording)
  • Key challenges to be aware of (for example transitions, anxiety, refusal, overwhelm)
  • Up to two non-negotiables (for example a must-include activity)

Using this information, I will select appropriate visuals and create a clear, child-appropriate order for each routine.

Important boundaries

This is a one-off personalised resource, not ongoing support.

  • No live calls
  • No ongoing coaching
  • No revisions beyond factual errors (for example a spelling mistake or missing detail)
  • Visual style and layout are fixed to keep routines clear and consistent
  • Turnaround time: up to 5 working days
