What this is

Animal Hugs are soft toy characters with a personalised printed T-shirt design. They are designed for animal lovers and make a thoughtful gift for birthdays, get well soon moments, “just because” surprises, or a comforting keepsake to take out and about.

Personalisation

  • Animal Hugs message (you can type your own wording at checkout)
  • Name printed on the T-shirt
  • Font, colour and optional icon chosen after selecting your bear style

Message ideas

Your message can be fully custom, but if you want a quick starting point, here are popular options based on the animal you choose. You will be able to type your exact wording after choosing your bear.

  • Classic bear: Bear Hugs / Brave Bear / Sleep Tight (or type your own)
  • Dog soft toy: Puppy Hugs / Best Friend / Paw Print (or type your own)
  • Lion soft toy: Brave Lion / Roar-some (or type your own)
  • Monkey soft toy: Cheeky Monkey / Monkey Hugs / Little Monkey (or type your own)

You will be able to type your exact message after choosing your bear.
