Bedtime Bear

A gentle personalised bear with a printed robe design, made to support calmer bedtime routines.

(22cm sitting height)

What this is

The Bedtime Bear is designed as a familiar comfort item for evenings and sleep routines. You can personalise the robe with a name and a calm, reassuring icon.

Personalisation

Personalise the robe with a name and an optional bedtime icon. For a calm, premium look we use a two-tone finish (accent colour + grey) by default.

What you will enter at checkout

  • Name: the name you want on the robe
  • Style: Font | Accent colour | Optional icon code | Any notes

Example:
Name: Charlie
Style: Soft rounded - Blue - C4

Defaults:
• Two-tone finish (accent colour + grey) for a calmer look.
• Icons print in your accent colour unless N1, N2, N3, N4, N5, N6 is chosen.
If you want everything in one colour, add: Single colour in your Style line.

Who this may suit

  • Children who like familiar bedtime comfort items
  • Families building predictable sleep routines
  • Gifts for new bedtime transitions

How it is used

Many families keep the bear as part of a predictable routine, for example: story time, cuddle, then bed. It can also be used as a calm comfort item for travel or overnight stays.
