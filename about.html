About Dotty Moo

Dotty Moo is a family-run UK brand creating personalised mascot bears and thoughtfully designed keepsakes that support connection, familiarity and a sense of belonging. We work with schools and organisations to create meaningful items, from mascot bears to simple, practical keepsakes, designed to reflect identity and be easy to introduce in a wide range of settings.

We keep things simple and supportive. Clear information, calm processes and thoughtful design are at the heart of everything we do.

Every name has a story

Dotty Moo began as a family nickname made up of “Dotty” and “Moo”, and grew into a brand inspired by childhood, connection, and the small moments that matter.

Everything we create is designed to feel kind and genuinely useful, whether it’s a mascot bear that marks a school memory, or a printable resource that supports routines at home.

What we care about

  • Thoughtful designs that feel personal to each setting
  • Clear, simple information for schools, families and organisations
  • Reliable production and carefully planned deliveries
  • Calm resources that support routines and wellbeing

How we work

For schools, there are two simple routes. Schools can run a parent ordering window using a private school-specific link, or place a direct bulk order for planned school use.

For organisations, we offer direct orders at wholesale pricing, usually starting with a sensible trial quantity and one clear bear design.

Our printable resources are designed to be calm and easy to use, with clear visuals and child-friendly language.
