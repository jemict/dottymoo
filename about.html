About Dotty Moo

We are a family-run business based in Worcestershire, creating high-quality keepsakes for schools across the UK.

Dotty Moo was founded by an education professional with over 20 years’ experience working across the education sector. Inspired by a love of creativity and community, Dotty Moo creates personalised mascot bears that help children and families celebrate their school identity with pride. Each bear is designed, printed, and packed with care, creating smiles, one cuddly keepsake at a time.

What we care about

  • Lovely designs that feel special to each school
  • Clear and simple ordering for parents
  • Reliable bulk deliveries direct to schools

How we work

Each design is carefully printed and finished using professional materials. Our team carefully prepares every school's order to ensure every bear arrives safely and ready to make pupils smile.

By ordering through your school, you help support activities and projects for pupils.
