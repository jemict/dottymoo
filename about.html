About Dotty Moo

Dotty Moo is a family-run UK brand creating personalised bears and keepsakes for children, families, schools and organisations. Each bear is designed to celebrate meaningful moments, offer comfort, or mark a special occasion.

Our direct-to-consumer range allows families to create a truly personal keepsake, with names, dates and short messages added to each bear. We keep the process clear and straightforward, with careful attention to presentation and quality.

Every name has a story

Dotty Moo began as a family nickname made up of “Dotty” and “Moo”, and grew into a brand inspired by childhood, connection and the small moments that matter.

We believe a simple, well-made item can carry a lot of meaning, especially when it is made just for someone.

What we care about

  • Thoughtful, personal designs that feel meaningful
  • Clear, honest information for families and organisations
  • Reliable production with realistic timescales
  • Warm, professional presentation from order to delivery

How we work

Families can order personalised bears directly through our shop, choosing the bear style and adding their own custom details.

Schools and organisations can place bulk orders at wholesale pricing. These are supplied as direct orders and are suitable for school offices, reading areas, events or gifting.

All school and organisational orders are handled directly with us to keep things simple and consistent.
