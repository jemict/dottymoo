Memorial Bears

A personalised keepsake bear with a printed T-shirt design, created to help you remember someone special.

Available on multiple bear styles. Choose your base below.

What this is

Memorial bears are designed as a gentle keepsake. You can add a short message, a photo on the T-shirt and a chosen icon.

It is not a medical product, but it can be a comforting item during grief, anniversaries, or moments of remembrance.

Personalisation

  • Short message Forever Loved, Always Remembered)
  • Photo design
  • optional icon chosen after selecting your bear style

Product details

  • Personalised printed T-shirt design
  • Made to order: your design is checked before printing
  • Safety: CE and UKCA marked, conforms to EN71 toy safety standards (base dependent)
