Comfort & Support Encouragement Bears

Personalised bears with a name on the front and a supportive message on the back, designed to offer comfort, reassurance and emotional support.

What this is

Comfort & Support Bears are personalised soft toy keepsakes designed to carry a gentle, reassuring message. Each bear includes a name printed on the front of the T-shirt and a supportive message on the back focused on comfort,kindness and feeling supported. They are designed as thoughtful gifts that help children feel reassured, understood and supported during difficult or uncertain moments.

Personalisation

  • Name printed on the front of the T-shirt
  • Comfort & support message printed on the back of the T-shirt
