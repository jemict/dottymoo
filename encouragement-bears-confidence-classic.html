What this is

Confidence & Encouragement Bears are personalised soft toy keepsakes designed to carry a supportive message. Each bear includes a name printed on the front of the T-shirt and a confidence or encouragement design printed on the back. They are designed as thoughtful gifts for reassurance, encouragement and everyday support.

How it works

  • Front of shirt: personalised with a name
  • Back of shirt: choose one Confidence & Encouragement design
  • Next step: select your base bear style below
