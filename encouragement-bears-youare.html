You Are Bears

Personalised bears with a name on the front and a positive affirmation on the back, designed to remind children of their strength, worth and potential.

What this is

You Are Bears are personalised soft toy keepsakes designed to carry a simple but powerful message. Each bear includes a name printed on the front of the T-shirt and a positive affirmation printed on the back beginning with “You are”. These bears are designed as thoughtful gifts that help build confidence, self-belief and encouragement through clear, uplifting messages.

Personalisation

  • Name printed on the front of the T-shirt
  • You Are message printed on the back of the T-shirt
