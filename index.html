How schools use mascot bears

Schools use Dotty Moo mascot bears in a variety of calm, meaningful ways. Some are given to leavers, some welcome new starters, and others become part of assemblies, reading routines, or school celebrations.

For pupils, mascot bears represent belonging and familiarity. For schools, they are a simple way to strengthen school identity without added workload.
