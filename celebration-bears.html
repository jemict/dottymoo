Celebration Bears

A personalised keepsake bear made for celebrations, milestones, and proud moments.

What this is

Celebration bears are designed for moments worth marking, such as birthdays, achievements, graduations, new jobs, first-day milestones, or personal wins.

Your wording is printed on the bear’s T-shirt to create a keepsake that feels personal and meaningful.

Personalisation

Each celebration bear includes a name and a celebration message printed on the T-shirt.

The exact layout, font, colour and optional icons are chosen on the next page after you select your bear style. This helps keep checkout simple and makes sure your personalisation is set up correctly.

Celebration message ideas

Your celebration message can be fully custom. Popular examples include:

  • Happy Birthday
  • Happy Easter
  • Wedding Day
  • First Day at School
  • Well done
  • Big Hugs
  • Valentines Hugs
  • I’m Here When You Need Me

You will be able to type your exact wording after choosing your bear.
