How to personalise at checkout

Checkout will ask for three lines. You can use them in any order.
For the best print size and clarity, we recommend keeping each line to around 20 characters. Longer lines may be resized to fit the available print area.

  • Line 1: name (styling notes in brackets)
  • Line 2: birth details (date/time/weight) (styling notes in brackets)
  • Line 3: optional newborn icon (styling notes in brackets)

Example:
Line 1: Olivia (script/pink)
Line 2: 12.01.26 03:42 7lb 6oz (handwritten/grey)
Line 3: B5 (icon-pink)

Please note: Anything written in brackets is for guidance only and will not be printed on the bear.

Your text will be printed exactly as entered.
Please check spelling, spacing, and capitalisation carefully before checkout.
